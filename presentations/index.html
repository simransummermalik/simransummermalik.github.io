CRA UR2PhD National Conference - New Orleans, LA

Date: April 24, 2026

I presented my hybrid classical-quantum machine learning work at the CRA UR2PhD National Conference in New Orleans, Louisiana.

Attending the CRA UR2PhD National Conference in New Orleans was one of the first times I truly felt like I belonged in the research community. I presented my quantum computing work, met other students and researchers from across the country, and had conversations about graduate school, DREU, and the future I could build in computing research.

I also loved the experience outside of the conference itself. The hotel was beautiful, they took such good care of us, and there was honestly so much food that I could not believe this was an opportunity I had earned. I am naturally very outgoing and curious, so I loved talking to new people, exploring the city, and hearing about everyone's work.

New Orleans was full of personality, culture, and energy, and I left feeling like I would genuinely love to live there one day.
