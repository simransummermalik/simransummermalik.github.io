Research Area: Quantum information science / quantum algorithms

Project Description

My summer research project focuses on studying quantum algorithms for integer factorization, especially Shor's algorithm and related improvements inspired by newer work on quantum factoring. The goal is to understand the algorithm from both the mathematical and implementation side by breaking down the quantum Fourier transform, period finding, circuit construction, simulation behavior, and the gap between theoretical speedups and realistic computational or hardware constraints.
