Research Area: Quantum information science and quantum algorithms

Project Description

My summer research project focuses on quantum algorithms for integer factorization, especially Shor's algorithm and newer approaches inspired by Regev's factoring framework. I am examining the problem from both the mathematical and implementation sides, including the quantum Fourier transform, period and relation finding, reversible modular arithmetic, multidimensional circuit construction, lattice-based classical post-processing, simulation behavior, and the gap between theoretical resource improvements and realistic computational or hardware constraints.
